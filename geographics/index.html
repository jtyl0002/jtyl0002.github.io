About - The world is interconnetced through a network of technologies. Personal devices, public CCTV and satelites accumulate information about the world it inhabits to build ever-growing, omnipresent artificial intlligence. In order to do this, humans are constantly being calibrated, recognised, monitored and tracked by computers.
Geographics is a 56 page collaboration of visual representations of the planetary-scale computation of modern society as outlined by Benjamin Bratton in his 2015 publication, The Stack.
